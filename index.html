Definitions Graphic

Communist

A system aiming for classless collective ownership of major productive property, often tied to centralized planning in historical state examples.

Socialist

A broad family of views favoring public, cooperative, social, or worker control over major economic resources and strong public services.

Democratic

A system based on popular sovereignty, competitive elections, civil liberties, rule of law, pluralism, and accountable institutions.

Fascist

An authoritarian ultranationalist ideology centered on centralized power, hierarchy, militarized unity, suppression of opposition, and state-directed society.

These are compact comparison definitions. Real people and parties can mix traits across categories.
